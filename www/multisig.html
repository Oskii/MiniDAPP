Multi Sig Wallet

Step 1 : You need to make you Keys.

Step 2 : Ask your friend for their PUBLIC KEY.. and paste it here. We'll create an address with 1 Minima that can only be spent if BOTH sign.

Step 4 : Now create the transaction that tries to spend those funds..
