HOME MIFI MINIDAPPS DEVELOPERS


The MiniDAPP HUB is accessed in your browser.

The site server is integrated directly into the Minima application.

If you are running Minima locally on this machine simply open http://127.0.0.1:21000/

If you are running it on your phone you can access it directly on your mobile (there is a button in the app to open the correct page ) or use the Phones' IP ( as shown in 'status' of the Minima Wallet or Terminal ) to access it on your desktop.

Don't worry. It's very simple.. but if this still sounds like gibberish just ask someone slightly more tecky than you..
