Minima MiFi

Welcome to the Minma MiFi demo.

DEFI Minima Style.

Here you will find some very simple demos that use the Minima MiFi Javscript library
which allow you to link a webpage to your phone or computer running Minima.

These are not complete apps. Just technical demos and examples of what is easily possible with a small static HTML+JS page.

[1] Super Simple Block Explorer The most basic building block. Connect your phone to the webpage and receieve data.
[2] Basic Wallet Let's pick up the pace and allow you to send some Minima..
[3] Terminal The original bad boy - The Terminal
[4] Script IDE A simple IDE for Minima scripts. Constantly Useful.
[5] Time Lock Funds Create a simple contract that locks funds away
[6] A Multi-Sig Contract An output that needs 2 people to spend
[7] Vault Wallet Hot wallet functionality with cold wallet security
[8] DEX Trade one token for another. Securely. Atomically
[9] Thunder Instant unlimited off-chain transactions.. Lightning by Minima..
[10] High Low Gambler 2 Player High low card game
[11] Poker Dice A full non-trivial state game
